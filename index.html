Biography

Academic Profile

A dedicated researcher and educator in the fields of Analysis and Algebra, committed to advancing mathematical knowledge through rigorous inquiry and inspiring the next generation of mathematicians.

Prof. Brahim Fahid is a Habilitated Professor of Mathematics at the Higher School of Technology, Ibn Tofaïl University, Kénitra, Morocco. His research focuses on algebraic structures, derivations, cohomology theory, path algebras, and residuated lattices — contributing original results to some of the most active frontiers of contemporary algebra.

He is a member of the Scientific Research and Innovation Laboratory under the direction of Prof. Habiba Chaoui, and has co-authored work with leading algebraists including Driss Bennis (Mohammed V University) and other international collaborators. His ORCID-verified publication record spans indexed international journals.
